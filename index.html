Research Projects

Brief descriptions of my research projects.

Distributed Reinforcement Learning for Smart Gird Energy System

This is a project which applies reinforcement learning methods for energy management. All data I use are from real-time historical log data from the open energy system in the hillside housing area at OIST campus site.

More

Emergence of communication among multi reinforcement learning agents

This research project use a decentralized reinforcement learning framework to understand how communication emerges in learning agents and the meaning evolved along the learning process.

More

Smartphone Robot

This is a project which I built robot platform with smartphones. The robots could be used for a variety of research topics, such as benchmarks for data-efficient reinforcement learning, and learning to cooperate with symbolic communication.
